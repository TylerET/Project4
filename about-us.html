Welcome to our cozy corner of the internet, where your furry, feathered, and finned friends are treated like family! I'm Kara, the heart and soul behind Pet's Under The Sun. My journey into pet sitting started from a deep-rooted love for animals and a desire to create a warm, nurturing environment for pets while their humans are away. With years of experience under my belt, I've cared for a wide range of pets, each with their own unique personalities and needs.

As a professional pet sitter, I understand the importance of trust and reliability. That's why I'm committed to providing not just a service, but a personalized experience for your pet. Whether it's their favorite playtime activity, a special diet, or a needed medication, I pay attention to the details that make your pet happy and healthy.

Safety and comfort are my top priorities. I'm certified in pet first aid and continually educate myself on the best practices in pet care. I believe in transparent communication, so you'll receive regular updates and photos, ensuring peace of mind while you're away.

Let's make your pet's staycation as joyful and loving as it can be. Because here, every wag, purr, and chirp is a language of love we speak fluently!
